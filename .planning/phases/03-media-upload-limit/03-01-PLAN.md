<objective>
Maximize media upload size limits so Postiz is never the bottleneck — platforms enforce their own limits on publish.

Purpose: Current limits are arbitrary (10MB images, 1GB videos) and lower than what major platforms accept (Instagram 30MB images, Facebook/YouTube 10GB+ videos). By raising to 100MB images and 10GB videos across all layers (backend validation, frontend Uppy, frontend session, nginx proxy), users can upload any media that their target platform would accept.
Output: Consistent limits across all upload layers: 100MB images, 10GB videos.
</objective>
